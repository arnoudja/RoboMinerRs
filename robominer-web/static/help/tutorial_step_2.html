Open the achievements page after a few mining runs from step 1. Your first achievement should be ready to claim.

Click Claim step. Common rewards include an extra mining queue slot, a higher wallet maximum for an ore type, or depot capacity so dump() at your spawn corner can bank ore during a rally.

Return to the mining queue and use Add to queue again. You can now queue multiple runs for the same robot, which makes it much easier to stay busy.

Check achievements regularly for new rewards.
